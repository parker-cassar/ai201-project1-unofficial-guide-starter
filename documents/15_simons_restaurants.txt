Title: Berkeley Restaurant Recommendations (Simons Institute)

Berkeley Restaurant Recommendations
Berkeley is home to some of the most famous places to eat in the Bay Area and nationwide.
Here are just a few suggestions below, most within a 2 mile radius from Simons. 
Many more great eats can be found in the greater Berkeley and Oakland areas - feel free to check out Yelp, Google Maps, or reach out to any of our staff for additional suggestions!
